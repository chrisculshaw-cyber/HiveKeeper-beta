We’re a small farm with limited parking — just 50 cars a day — so please book in advance to be sure of your spot. It’s just £1 per car and everyone in the car is free. Payment is handled securely by SumUp.

Before you visit — please read

Nene Valley Lavender is a working farm. The ground is uneven and there may be holes, ditches, mud, slopes, water, farm machinery and animals. Sturdy footwear is strongly advised and suitable clothing for the weather. Please supervise children at all times and keep dogs on leads. Visitors enter and explore the farm at their own risk; please take care and follow any signs or instructions from staff on the day.

Our Story

Calm, grown by hand

Our aim is simple — to create products that relax you.

We want to give you an experience of calming wellbeing when you come and wander around our lavender fields. Lavender has been used for centuries for many remedies, and wandering among our plants is extremely peaceful.

Everything we make — the essential oil, the skincare blends, the honey and beeswax from our own hives — comes from this one small farm in the stunning Nene Valley, made to promote both mental and body wellbeing.
