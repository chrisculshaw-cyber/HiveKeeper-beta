HiveKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

HiveKeeper is built by Chris Culshaw / Culshaw Consulting Ltd. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What data we collect

When you use HiveKeeper, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you register
  • Apiary and hive data — hive names, locations, inspection records, varroa counts, harvest records, treatment records
  • Photos — frame photos and hive cover photos you choose to upload. These are stored in Supabase cloud storage.
  • Location — your apiary location (postcode or town name) for weather and Asian Hornet Watch. GPS location is only used for the SOS emergency feature and is never stored.
  • Voice recordings — audio recorded during inspections is sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription. We do not store the audio file — only the resulting transcript.

How we use your data

  • To provide the HiveKeeper service — storing and syncing your beekeeping records across devices
  • To generate your calendar subscription — inspection reminders and seasonal events
  • To power AI features — frame photo analysis via Claude Vision (Anthropic) and voice transcription via Whisper (OpenAI)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Third party services

  • Supabase — database and file storage (supabase.com)
  • OpenAI Whisper — voice transcription. Audio is processed and immediately discarded (openai.com)
  • Anthropic Claude — AI frame photo analysis. Images are processed and not retained (anthropic.com)
  • Open-Meteo — weather data. No personal data is shared (open-meteo.com)
  • Cloudflare — API proxy and Worker hosting (cloudflare.com)

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Your rights

You can:

  • Export all your data at any time via Settings → Download data
  • Delete all your data via Settings → Start fresh
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing chris@culshaw.consulting

Children

HiveKeeper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified in the app.
